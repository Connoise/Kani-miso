# Contradiction Handling

## Purpose

Contradictions are expected and preserved in this system.

A contradiction indicates:
- change over time
- contextual difference
- unresolved tension
- evolving understanding

They are not errors.

---

## Rules

- Do not reconcile contradictions automatically
- Do not delete older interpretations
- Do not label one version as correct

Contradictions should coexist.

---

## Allowed Actions

- Link contradictory notes together
- Add a note acknowledging the tension
- Create or use a hub that can hold multiple viewpoints

---

## Forbidden Actions

- Selecting a “better” interpretation
- Merging contradictory notes
- Normalizing differences into vague consensus

---

## Default Stance

Preserve tension.
Let time resolve meaning, not automation.
