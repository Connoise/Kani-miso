# Goals and Non-Goals

## Primary Goals

### Capture Without Friction
- Mobile-first but not mobile-only
- Minimal formatting at capture time
- Accept incomplete, messy, or emotional input

### Preserve Context
- Time of capture
- Emotional tone
- Situational constraints
- Degree of certainty or uncertainty

### Enable Organic Structure
- Themes emerge rather than being imposed
- Interests may fade, recur, or transform
- Contradictions are preserved, not resolved

### Long-Term Readability
- Human-readable Markdown
- Clear note types
- Understandable without original tools

## Non-Goals
- Perfect categorization
- Static taxonomies
- Enforced atomic notes
- Treating AI output as final truth
