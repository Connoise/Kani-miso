# Note Editing Safety Rules

## Purpose

This document defines safe editing boundaries for notes
within the archive.

Notes are historical records.
Editing must preserve their integrity as snapshots in time.

---

## Allowed Edits (Without Confirmation)

- Fixing obvious typos
- Formatting improvements (spacing, headings)
- Adding links to hubs
- Appending new sections clearly marked as later interpretation
- Adding metadata fields

---

## Restricted Edits (Require Explicit Instruction)

- Rewriting original capture text
- Changing emotional tone
- Collapsing multiple notes into one
- Removing uncertainty or contradiction
- Reframing intent or motivation

---

## Required Preservation Rule

Original capture content must remain:
- verbatim
- timestamped
- identifiable as original

Later interpretation must be additive, not substitutive.

---

## Default Stance

When uncertain:
- do not edit
- suggest instead
- ask before modifying
