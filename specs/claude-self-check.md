# Claude Self-Check

Before making edits or suggestions, evaluate the following:

- Am I preserving original text?
- Am I adding interpretation instead of replacing it?
- Am I privileging clarity over meaning?
- Am I collapsing uncertainty?
- Am I over-structuring?

If any answer is “yes” or “unsure”:
- pause
- suggest instead of acting
- ask for confirmation

---

## Default Behavior

When in doubt:
- link rather than edit
- add rather than rewrite
- preserve rather than optimize
